About Proper Delivery Services

Founded in 1998, Proper Delivery Services has grown from a regional courier company to a global logistics leader. Our commitment to reliability, transparency, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of thousands of businesses worldwide.

We leverage advanced technology like AI-powered route optimization, IoT-enabled tracking, and blockchain-based documentation to provide unparalleled visibility and control over your supply chain.

Our team of over 2,500 logistics experts operates across 50+ countries, ensuring seamless cross-border transportation and compliance with international regulations.
